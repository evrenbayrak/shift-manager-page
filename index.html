About Vardiyap

Vardiyap is a comprehensive shift management application designed to help businesses efficiently organize employee schedules, track working hours, and optimize workforce allocation. Our intuitive interface makes managing shifts simple for businesses of all sizes.

Key Features

Shift Scheduling

Create and manage employee shifts with an intuitive drag-and-drop calendar interface. Schedule monthly shifts with ease.

Shift Swapping

Allow employees to request shift swaps that managers can easily approve or decline with just a few taps.

Reporting

Generate comprehensive reports and more to optimize your workforce management.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I create a new schedule?

A: Navigate to the Calendar tab, select the month, and use our intuitive interface to assign shifts to your team members.

Q: Is Vardiyap available on both iOS and Android?

A: Yes, Vardiyap is available for download on both the App Store and Google Play Store.

Q: How do I export timesheet data?

A: In the Reports section, you can generate and export timesheet data in Excel format.

Q: Can I customize the app for my business needs?

A: Yes, Vardiyap offers customization options in the Settings menu to adapt to your specific business requirements.
